Bringing mobile web users to you

QR codes connect the physical world to mobile phones. They are a kind of pixel-based "square bar code" that is readable by the camera in most smart phones and tablet PCs and take you to a web page. What's special about Q-Action is that while the printed code is fixed, the page it shows is up-datable and re-configurable. Q-Action pages can give you:

  • Electronic business cards and leaflets that never go out of date
  • Mini web sites that you manage from your mobile
  • Electronic posters for nearby services, showing current offers
  • Dynamic codes that can point to different web sites at different times
  • Window offers with a "buy now" facility for shops, estate agents, notice boards
  • Everlasting membership cards that always show the member's current status
  • Warranty and service cards with up-datable product and installation info
  • Stock control cards that you can update centrally or on location
  • Emergency advice notices, contact information, and nearest help
  • Rota lists that show who or what is available today
  • Up-to-the-minute live programme guides for events and shows
  • Location pages that show location and facility status or visitor guides
  • All the other ideas we haven't thought of but you will

You control your Q-Action pages from any browser. Users scan your printed tag using any standard QR code reader app. They can then take immediate action - browse, buy, visit, call, or just read and learn.
